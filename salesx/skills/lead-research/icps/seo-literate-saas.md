# ICP 1 — SEO-Literate, AI-Using SaaS Teams & Founders

SaaS teams and founders who already run real SEO work, already use AI tools to do it, and
where the buyer personally understands SEO/AEO well enough to operate a platform like
Berlin themselves.

## Must-haves (the ICP)

- **Vertical:** SaaS.
- **Revenue:** 80k – 200k MRR (800k – 25 million ARR). *(see note below)*
- **Active SEO:** Already doing real SEO work — not starting from zero.
- **AI usage:** Already using AI tools like Claude to do the SEO work or marketing work.
- **Team shape:** A small SEO/marketing team, or a founder doing the SEO themselves.
- **Buyer literacy:** The owner (founder or marketing leader) personally understands
  SEO/AEO well — a literate buyer who can operate a platform.

## Derived — disqualifiers (edit freely; I inferred these from the must-haves)

The competitor exclusion is already enforced by the skill's competitive-conflict gate; the
following are the practical inversions of the criteria above:

- Not SaaS.
- Not actually doing SEO yet (starting from zero) — no existing content/SEO motion to build on.
- No AI in their marketing/SEO workflow.
- A buyer who wants a done-for-you agency rather than to operate a self-serve platform.

## Derived — how to score (edit freely)

- Strongest fits hit all six must-haves: SaaS, in the revenue band, real existing SEO,
  AI-using, small/founder-led team, and a literate buyer.
- Treat **buyer literacy + active SEO + AI usage** as the core thesis — they're what makes
  Berlin land. A SaaS in the revenue band that *doesn't* already do SEO, or isn't
  AI-literate, is a weaker fit even when the firmographics match.
