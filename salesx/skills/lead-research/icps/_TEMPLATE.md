## Who we are
What we sell and what we replace (so the skill can judge relevance and run the
competitive-conflict gate). e.g. "Berlin — an AI SEO/AEO platform that replaces hiring an
SEO agency or in-house SEO team."

## Who we target (must-haves)
The segment, business model, and behavioral signals a good-fit company shows.
- Segment / business model: …
- Company size: … (employees and/or revenue band)
- Geography / language: …
- Behavioral signals of fit: … (e.g. runs its own content/SEO motion, has a real content
  footprint with room to improve, marketing/growth-led)

## Disqualifiers
What makes a target a "no" regardless of other signals.
- … (the competitor exclusion is already enforced by the skill's competitive-conflict
  gate — add anything beyond it here: too large/entrenched, no organic-search motion,
  out of geo/language, etc.)

## Decision-maker
Who to find and reach out to. e.g. "Head of Marketing / Growth / SEO, or a founder at
smaller companies."

## Notes for scoring
Any calibration guidance — what "strong" vs "partial" vs "weak" looks like for this ICP,
thresholds to weigh, segments to treat leniently/strictly.
